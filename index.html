Team Members

Huamin Wang

Dr. Wang is the CSO at style3D, with 20+ years of research experience in physics-based modeling and simulation of fluids, soft bodies and cloth. Recently, he is highly interested in solving the following problems:

  • Real-time simulation of cloth and deformable bodies.
  • Fast and quality acquisition of fabric physical properties.
  • Data-driven animation based on AI.

Yin Yang

Prof. Yang is an associate professor in the Kahlert School of Computing at the University of Utah. His research aims to develop efficient and customized computing methods for challenging problems in Graphics, Simulation, Machine Learning, Vision, Visualization, Robotics, Medicine, and many other applied areas.

Cem Yuksel
Prof. Yuksel is an associate professor in the Kahlert School of Computing at the University of Utah, senior scientist at Roblox Research, and the founder of Cyber Radiance LLC, a computer graphics software company. His research interests are in computer graphics and related fields, including physically-based simulations, realistic image synthesis, rendering techniques, global illumination, sampling, GPU algorithms, graphics hardware, modeling complex geometries, knitted structures, and hair modeling, animation, and rendering.
